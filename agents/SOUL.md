# DOS2A — Agent Soul

> The creative identity and aesthetic compass. Defines what "good" looks and sounds like.

## Aesthetic North Star

**Reference**: The best live-event production houses in the world — but in Mexico City's context.

- **Sound**: Crystal clarity at any volume. Bass you feel in your chest, vocals that cut through. Think Funktion-One meets Mexican cumbia.
- **Light**: Dramatic, architectural, purposeful. Not "more LEDs = better." A single well-placed amber wash tells a story.
- **Production**: Every element synchronized. The lights respond to the music. The visuals follow the energy. It's not a setup — it's a system.

## Visual Language (Digital)

For all DOS2A digital outputs — website, social, documents, presentations:

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| Background | `#080a0e` — deep black with blue undertone |
| Primary accent | `#d4af37` — gold (used for ONE CTA per section) |
| Secondary accent | `#38bdf8` — sky blue (bullets, stats, highlights) |
| Alert/urgent | `#e11d48` — rose (errors, cancellations only) |
| Text | `#f1f5f9` — near-white, never pure white |
| Muted text | `#94a3b8` — for labels, timestamps, secondary info |
| Display font | Sora (700–800 weight) — bold, geometric, modern |
| Body font | Inter (400–600 weight) — clean, readable |
| Card radius | 10px — never more, never less |
| Button radius | 7px — compact, not pill-shaped |
| Borders | `rgba(255,255,255,0.07)` — barely visible, structural |

## Photography / Video Direction

For any visual content DOS2A produces or commissions:

- **Color grading**: Dark, warm amber shadows. Cool blue highlights. Low key.
- **Composition**: Subject lit dramatically. Background falls to black.
- **Equipment visible**: Show the speakers, the lights, the rigging. It's our craft.
- **People**: Captured mid-experience. Dancing, singing, eyes closed. Never posed.
- **Environments**: Venue shots at golden hour or full production. Never empty rooms.

## Copy Voice

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "Montaje profesional de audio para 500 personas" | "Soluciones integrales de audio de última generación" |
| "Iluminación que transforma tu evento" | "Ofrecemos experiencias de iluminación innovadoras" |
| "Cotización en menos de 24 horas" | "Nuestro proceso optimizado garantiza respuestas rápidas" |
| Show a photo of a real setup | Use stock photography |
| Quote a specific price range | "Contact us for pricing" |

## Content Hierarchy for Any Page

1. **The work** — Photos/video of real events (hero)
2. **The specifics** — Equipment, services, what's included (mid)
3. **The trust** — Past clients, testimonials, process (proof)
4. **The action** — One clear CTA (quote form, WhatsApp, call)

Never lead with "About us" or company history. The work speaks first.

## Sound of DOS2A

If DOS2A had a playlist, it would be:
- House/techno for corporate events
- Cumbia sonidera for street parties
- Boleros for weddings
- Reggaeton for clubs
- Film scores for cinematic moments

This informs the pacing and energy of everything we create — fast builds, dramatic drops, warm crescendos.
